Privacy Policy

How we protect and manage your data.

At Agrolytix, protecting your privacy and the data of your agribusiness is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines what information we collect, why we collect it, and how it is protected.

We collect information you provide directly to us when you create an account, build your inventory, or communicate with us. This includes your name, email, business details, and inventory data.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to operate, maintain, and improve the Agrolytix platform. We do not sell your personal or business data to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.
